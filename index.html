uO Campus Guide

An app to help students at the University of Ottawa learn more about their campus, get involved, and stay organized.

Find

Search through a list of all the buildings on and off campus to find information on any room you’re looking for!

Need directions? With your location, or indicating your current location on campus, we’ll give you step by step instructions to get there.

Discover

Learn more about your campus than you thought you ever would with the discovery section.

Find tonnes of information on the school, faculties, events, and even city transit.

Schedule

If you're in need of a convenient place to keep track of your weekly, then look no further than uO Campus Guide.

Setup reminders for courses, events on campus, and get step-by-step directions to all of them.

Search

Never feel overwhelmed by the app and all it's great features.

There's a convenient location for you to search ALL the content, from a single page, and browse for exactly what you're interested in.

Settings

Make the app fit your needs

You can check the settings to switch the language, access various accessibility features, and report any issues you encounter so we can make it better for you!
